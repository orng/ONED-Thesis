In this chapter we will look at the results of the experiments discussed in chapter~\ref{chapter:experiments}.

\Crefrange{tab:d1}{tab:d4} show the ratio of articles which resulted in no new words, no new pairs or both. In the cases where filtering was applied a shorthand for the filter type (cf=collection frequency, tf=term frequency) is included as well as the threshold. We can note that filtering does in fact increase the number of empty output-sets of either size.
